Agra Special

Places to see, ways to wander, and signature experiences, The city of the Taj Mahal, one of the seven wonders of the world, Agra in Uttar Pradesh is India's most popular tourist destination. Most famous monuments here are Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, Tomb of Itidmulah, Sheesh Mahal, Mehtab Bagh, Kalakirti Cultural and Convention Center.

Situated on the banks of River Yamuna, the city, once the capital of the mighty Mughals, indulge in contemporary delicacies at upscale restaurants like Good Vibes Cafe, Taj Terrace, Tea'se Me, The Salt Cafe Kitichen aans Bar, The Grand Gopika Thaal, Dostea Cafe, Taj Bano, Bambino Cafe and Restaurants, D'Delicia and Yes Restaurent

gra finds a mention in the epic Mahabharata, where it is referred to as ‘Agraban’ or an integral part of the Braj Bhumi or the land of Lord Krishna. A lot of significant historical events in the city are said to have unfolded during the reign of Raja Badal Singh, a Sikarwar Rajput king, who is believed to have founded the city in 1475.

For a mix of the charming, modern, and tried and true stay visitors can also check into luxurious hotels Holiday Inn, The Oberoi Amarvilas, Trident, Ekka Villa, Sterling Agra, Double Tree by Hilton Hotel, Courtyard Agra, ITC Mughal The Coral Court and many more are available.
